Hobbies

Dance

My hobby is Dance. I feel joyful and energized whenever I perform. It is not just an art but also a great exercise for the body. Dance improves posture and builds confidence. I have been learning classical dance since I was in 6th grade. I also love contemporary and hip-hop styles. Dance helps me express emotions and creativity. My favorite form is Bharatanatyam(South Indian classical), and I have performed in many school events. Recently, I won a prize in a competition where I had earlier lost. It gave me a new boost. Dance, in short, is not just my hobby, it's my P&E.

Listening to music

My favorite hobby is listening to music. It makes me feel relaxed and helps me focus better. I love exploring different genres like pop, classical, and lo-fi. Music can instantly uplift my mood when I'm feeling low. I often listen to songs while studying, especially during late night sessions. I prefer instrumental music while working and soft beats while resting. Music has the power to heal and energize. I recently started making playlists for different moods, which I used to ignore earlier. One of my favorite artists is T.S., and her songs always inspire me. I also enjoy lyrics that have deep meanings written in simple words.
